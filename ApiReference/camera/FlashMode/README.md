## Namespace: "camera".FlashMode
Specifies a camera flash mode.

##### Variables
 - **on** - _String_.    
  The camera flash is disabled.
 - **off** - _String_.    
  Flash will be fired automatically when required.
 - **auto** - _String_.    
  The camera flash is enabled.
